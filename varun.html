How It Works

We've designed our app to make finding a good roommate as easy as possible! On our survey, there are various questions about lifestyle habits. For each of these, you will answer them, put what your ideal roommate should answer, and place an importance on this factor in your life. Our matching algorithm will compare your responses to everyone else's and develop compatibility scores. Then you can see whom you match with the best!
